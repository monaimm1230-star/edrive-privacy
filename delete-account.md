# Delete Your E-Drive Account

To request deletion of your account and all associated data:

1. Email us at: support@edrive.energy
2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
3. Include your registered email address in the email

We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days including:
- Your profile (name, email)
- Your trade history
- Your energy offers and requests
- Your wallet balance
- Your chat messages

We may retain transaction records on our blockchain as these are immutable by design.
