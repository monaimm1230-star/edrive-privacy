# Privacy Policy for E-Drive Energy

**Last updated: May 9, 2026**

## Introduction

E-Drive Energy ("we", "our", or "us") operates the E-Drive mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
- Email address (used for login and identification)
- Full name (displayed on your profile)
- Password (stored as an encrypted hash, never in plain text)

**Location Data**
- GPS coordinates when you use the App, used to show nearby energy traders and calculate distances between buyers and sellers
- We do not store your location history

**Energy Trading Data**
- Energy offers and requests you post (packets, price, location)
- Trade history and transaction records
- Wallet balance (E-Credits)

**Device Information**
- Push notification token (used to send trade alerts)
- Device platform (Android/iOS)

## How We Use Your Information

- To create and manage your account
- To match buyers and sellers based on location and requirements
- To record energy trades on our blockchain ledger
- To send push notifications about completed trades and offers
- To display your wallet balance and transaction history
- To improve the App's performance and features

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on:
- **MongoDB Atlas** — cloud database with encryption at rest
- **Render** — secure cloud hosting for our backend servers
- **Blockchain** — immutable trade records on our private blockchain

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:
- Service providers who help us operate the App (MongoDB, Render)
- When required by law or legal process

Your email address is partially masked (e.g. `john...`) when shown to other users on the platform.

## Location Data

Location is only accessed when you are actively using the App (foreground only). We use your location to:
- Show nearby energy sellers and buyers on the map
- Calculate distances between traders
- Display your approximate area on your listings

We do not track your location in the background.

## Push Notifications

We send push notifications to inform you about:
- Completed energy trades
- New matching offers or requests

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Encrypted password storage
- Blockchain-based immutable transaction records
- Secure API authentication

## Children's Privacy

E-Drive Energy is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt out of push notifications
- Request a copy of your transaction history

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email.
